Australian koala

Koalas aren't bears-they are marsupials!! and Baby koals are cute and also they are fussy eaters!!

  • Scientific Name:: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Average weigth:: 4 to 15 Kgs
  • Average Lifespan:: 13-18 years
  • Kingdom:: Animalia

The Koala or,inaccurately, koala bear is an arboreal herbivourous marsupial native to australia. It is the only extent representative of the family phascolarctidae and its a closest living relatives are the wombats.Koalas are 2 to 3 feet tall when fully grown.
